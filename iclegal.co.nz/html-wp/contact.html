How We Help Our Clients

Follow these steps
to start your process

Choosing the right representative is the first step towards a safe, pleasant and fortunate immigration path. Years of experience and a solid moral foundation are the best warranty we can offer.
Step 1

Book the initial appointment

This appointment is a paid appointment, and it is the first step towards securing our representation.
Step 2

Meet your certified consultant

You´ll learn the best immigration path, including program, province, budget, documents, and timeline.
Step 3

Upload all your documentation

We will create a safe personal file in our servers for you, protected with a username and password, for this purpose.

Ready to submit your application

We will process your file promptly, and then we will submit it to Immigration Canada for approval.
